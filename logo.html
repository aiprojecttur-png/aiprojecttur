Rhombix — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026  |  Effective date: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Project TUR ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use Rhombix ("the Game"), a mobile reflex game available on Google Play.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We designed Rhombix to collect the minimum amount of data necessary for core game functionality.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Google Play Games Services Data

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games Services, we access the following information provided by Google:

  • Display Name — Your Google Play Games public display name, shown on leaderboards.
  • Player ID — A unique identifier assigned by Google Play Games Services, used to associate your scores with your account.
We do not access your email address, real name, contacts, photos, Google account password, or any other personal information from your Google account.

1.2 Game Data (Stored Locally)

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • High scores and game history
  • Game settings and preferences (theme, sound, colorblind mode, rhombus model)
  • Gameplay statistics (total matches, reaction times, play time)
  • Skill rating (GSR — Game Skill Rating)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • ❌ Email addresses
  • ❌ Real names or personal identifiers
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • ❌ Contacts or phone numbers
  • ❌ Photos, files, or media
  • ❌ Financial or payment information
  • ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we access is used exclusively for:

  • Leaderboards — Submitting and displaying your scores on Google Play Games leaderboards so you can compete with other players globally.
  • Profile display — Showing your Play Games display name within the Game.
  • Game functionality — Storing your preferences and progress locally on your device for a seamless gameplay experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

When you submit a score to the leaderboard, your display name and score are shared with Google Play Games Services and become visible to other players on the public leaderboard. This is a core feature of Google Play Games Services and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All game data (settings, scores, statistics) is stored locally on your device using the app's local storage.
  • Leaderboard data is stored and managed by Google Play Games Services on Google's secure servers.
  • We do not operate our own servers or databases that store your personal data.
  • All communication with Google Play Games Services is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all game data is stored locally on your device:

  • Clearing app data or uninstalling the Game will permanently delete all locally stored data.
  • You can sign out of Google Play Games from the Game's settings at any time.
  • To delete your Google Play Games data (leaderboard scores, etc.), please visit your Google Play Games settings or refer to Google's data deletion guide.

6. Children's Privacy

Rhombix is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to address the issue.

7. Third-Party Services

Rhombix integrates with the following third-party service:

  • Google Play Games Services — For authentication, leaderboards, and achievements. Google's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

8. Permissions

Rhombix requests the following Android permission:

  • INTERNET — Required for connecting to Google Play Games Services (leaderboards and authentication).

No other special permissions are requested.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
